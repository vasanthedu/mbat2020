Get ready for the 30th MBA Tournament

The HEC MBA Tournament, or MBAT, is an annual sporting event that brings together MBA students from top-ranked business schools around the world.

Organized by MBA students of HEC Paris, a leading business school in Europe, the tournament is the continent's largest annual gathering of MBA students. Over 1500 international future business leaders visit our campus every May for heated competition, friendly rivalries, and passionate celebrations.

MBAT consists of sporting competitions, festivities, and cultural exchange - all taking place on the beautiful HEC Paris campus and at select venues in the vicinity of Paris.

Dean's message

The MBAT is always one of the major highlights of the calendar at HEC Paris MBA and is the biggest gathering of MBA students in Europe. Given the challenges that we have faced over the last months, we have completely redesigned the MBAT this year, while still keeping the core of the competition. We are very excited to welcome you to the first ever virtual MBAT.

While the format of the MBAT will be different this year, its spirit, and the values it represents remain unchanged. The three days of MBAT unite over 1500 participants from around the world, making it an unparalleled opportunity to network, exchange cultures, and foster lifelong connections. The MBAT is a unique event, an opportunity to exercise your teamwork and leadership skills, as well as your sporting prowess.

We look forward to sharing this fantastic experience with you. Let's get together again.
